Data Protection

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

Introduction

Welcome to SmartAlloc. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform or use our emergency allocation and volunteer matching services.

Information We Collect

To provide our rapid-response services, we collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Identification Data: Full name, email address, and phone number during account registration.
  • Geolocation Data: Real-time GPS location (only when you actively submit a help request or switch your status to "Active Volunteer").
  • Service Data: Details of the emergency or help required, skill sets of volunteers, and communication logs within the platform.

How We Use Your AI Data

SmartAlloc utilizes a Google Gemini-powered AI matching engine. When a request is submitted, the AI analyzes the problem category and location to find the optimal volunteer. Your data is processed strictly for matching purposes. We do not use your private emergency details or location history to train external public AI models, nor do we sell this data to third-party marketing agencies.

Data Sharing & Disclosure

Your exact location and contact number remain encrypted and hidden by default. They are only unlocked and shared with a specific, verified volunteer *after* they have officially accepted your active request. We may also disclose your information to law enforcement agencies if required by Indian Law (e.g., under the IT Act, 2000) to prevent fraud or investigate severe misuse.

DPDP Act 2023 Compliance

In accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 of India, you have the absolute right to access, correct, or erase your personal data. You may request the deletion of your account and all associated historical data at any time through your dashboard settings or by contacting our Data Protection Officer.

Data Retention & Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect your data. Geo-location data linked to a specific emergency request is anonymized and archived 30 days after the request is resolved. Account credentials and personal identifiers are stored using secure hashing algorithms.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our technology or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
