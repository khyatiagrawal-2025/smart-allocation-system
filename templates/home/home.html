How It Works

Three steps to the right help

Our intelligent pipeline connects you to the most suitable volunteer in under a minute.

📋 1

Submit Request

Describe your need — type of help, urgency level, and your location. Takes under 60 seconds.

Features

Everything you need to
get help at scale

Smart Matching Algorithm

Multi-factor scoring weighs skills, experience, location, urgency, and past performance to surface the perfect volunteer.

Real-Time Availability

Volunteer status is tracked live. Only available, online volunteers appear in results — no wasted time on unavailable contacts.

Multi-Level Allocation

Searches expand intelligently: Local → City → Global. If no local volunteer is found, the system widens the net automatically.

Urgency Prioritization

High-urgency requests jump the queue. Critical needs are always met first with priority routing.
